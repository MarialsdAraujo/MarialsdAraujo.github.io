Data Analyst

Greetings!
My name is Maria Luiza, a Business Analytics student who sees data as more than just numbers. For me, every dataset has a hidden story, and I love the challenge of discovering it. My journey into analytics didn’t start with formulas and spreadsheets, but with curiosity. I’ve always wanted to understand why things happen and how patterns can guide better decisions. That curiosity grew into skills in SQL, Python, and data visualization, helping me turn complex information into clear and meaningful insights.

My vision
I believe data should be approachable and inspiring. My goal is to connect facts with context, making analytics not only useful but engaging, a way to bring clarity where there’s complexity and to spot details that others might overlook.

My experience
Along the way, I’ve worked on projects that blended technical precision with creativity, from building interactive dashboards to shaping data-driven strategies. Each one taught me something new and helped me see the bigger picture of how analytics can create impact.

Working with me
Collaboration is where the magic happens. I enjoy working with people who share my curiosity, turning data into informed decisions and decisions into actionable outcomes. Whether it’s exploring a dataset, designing a dashboard, or brainstorming strategy, I aim to keep things both insightful and enjoyable.

Let's Connect
If you’re here, we probably share the belief that data can change how we see the world. I’d love to exchange ideas, stories, and possibilities. Feel free to reach out, and let’s turn data into something amazing together!

With curiosity and creativity, Malu Araújo
